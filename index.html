What we can do for you

Learning Tapestry provides

Product design & implementation

Our highly expert product designers, software engineers and technology principals will identify your solutions needs in a way that can be quickly and cost-effectively implemented.

Tech Design &
management

Consulting team will provide you with outside perspective on the product and development strategy, or review your development methods and code base to find ways to improve your systems and answer questions.

Education tech integration

We help partners integrate technology infrastructure into existing systems, help organizations with limited technical resources to get the most out of their existing products.

CTO on demand services

Our consulting CTOs can provide high-end, cost-effective help for small organizations who aren’t ready for a full-time CTO, and for larger organizations who have CTOs that are already committed to other priority projects.

We build & operate infrastructure components

Learning Tapestry builds and operates infrastructure components with new capabilities on top of existing education systems. These components help provide snap-in digital content search and analytics functions in learning environments.
