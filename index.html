What Learning Tapestry Can Do for You

Product Development & Implementation

Our multidisciplinary teams will work with you to understand your objectives and identify technical solutions that meet your needs, and we will deliver on time and within budget.

Technology Consulting

Our technology leaders can provide you with CTO-level strategic consulting services if your organization is too small to have a full-time CTO or if your CTO needs added support.

Systems Integrations

Our backend development engineers will architect and implement custom integrations between disparate systems to help you get the most from your existing products.

Customizable Solutions for Content Creators

Our LCMS, bulk downloader, and AI content generator tools are designed specifically for educational content creators so you can create and distribute your content more efficiently.

We help Noggin with their entire technology platform.

From technology strategy, software architecture, devops, mobile development, web development, to analytics/learning data infrastructure: we are either lead in each of these areas or provide key technology support. Our partnership with Noggin has grown over the years as we've demonstrated the ability to solve hard problems, identify areas in need of attention, and think through technical problems from the point of view of technology architecture, product vision, business requirements, and marketing goals.

We've helped Noggin migrate completely from a codebase that slowed their innovation to one that is meeting their needs for creating the kid-friendly, learning-rich experience they want for their young users and their families. And we achieved all this on-time, on-budget, and with a reliable and performant infrastructure.

Learning Tapestry provides us both outstanding technology strategy and leadership, as well as a highly talented technical team. We confidently rely on Learning Tapestry to help us build our early childhood mobile and web products.

Kristen Kane

Executive Vice President

Noggin, a division of Paramount

We helped OpenSciEd build an open source curriculum authoring environment

It enables them to create and maintain all the open educational resource (OER) curriculum products they provide to schools.

We also provide integration support to connect their curriculum products with their public website.

Learning Tapestry's open source curriculum tools (LCMS) have transformed the way that we author materials and distribute final products to teachers. The ability to offer our materials in new and upcoming delivery platforms without having to revisit our authored content changes the way we think about portability. Working with Learning Tapestry is easy because we share the values embodied in providing the market with high quality free and open products.

Jim Ryan

OpenSciEd
