Clients

Learning Tapestry has partnered with many organizations across the education sector, including: The US Department of Education, The Australian National Government, The California Department of Education, The Georgia Department of Education, Amazon.com, Google, Stanford University, New York University.

Among our clients are Salem-Keizer School District, MacArthur Foundation, The Walton Family Foundation, Amplify Education, Pearson and many more.
Here're some selected references for our work.

WE HELPED UNBOUNDED CREATE DIGITAL-FIRST CURRICULA AND COLLABORATION TOOLS

UnboundEd is dedicated to empowering teachers by providing free, high-quality standards-aligned resources for the classroom, the opportunity for immersive training through their Standards Institute events, and the option of support through their website services.

Their online curriculum resources for grades PreK-12 have been reviewed and sequenced by nationally-recognized math and ELA experts. Their professional learning experiences and resources, help educators integrate standards-based knowledge and content into their daily practice.

Learning Tapestry is a key partner for our work. They help us design, build, and support the technology that drives our work in content and curriculum.

Laura Smith

Managing Partner, UnboundEd

We helped Relay transition to sustainable learning infrastructure

Relay wanted to transition from home-grown learning and finance systems to more stable infrastructure. We helped them navigate through this challenging process.

Along the way, we also helped them redesign their internal technical organization. We also help them with internal R&D work, including Doug Lamov's innovative Teach Like a Champion program (tlaconline.com).

Learning Tapestry has provided us with strategic insight into technology that we couldn't find anywhere else.

Norman Atkins

Co-founder and President, Relay GSE
